# Speddy: Feature Overview for School Districts

**Your Complete Special Education Management Platform**

---

## What is Speddy?

Speddy is an all-in-one platform designed specifically for special education teams. It helps resource specialists, special education assistants (SEAs), speech-language pathologists, occupational therapists, counselors, and teachers manage their caseloads, schedules, and student progress—all in one place.

---

## Who Uses Speddy?

| Role                                    | What Speddy Helps Them Do                                                               |
| --------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Resource Specialists**                | Manage caseloads, schedule sessions, generate lesson materials, track IEP goal progress |
| **Special Education Assistants (SEAs)** | View assigned students, follow schedules, track curriculum progress                     |
| **Speech Therapists & OTs**             | Manage specialized caseloads, create targeted materials, document progress              |
| **Teachers**                            | View which students receive services, understand schedules, coordinate with specialists |
| **Site & District Administrators**      | Oversee staff, manage teacher accounts, view school-wide data                           |

## Core Features

### 1. Student Caseload Management

Easily manage all students receiving special education services.

**What you can do:**

- Add students individually or import in bulk via CSV
- Track student information including grade level, teacher, and service details
- Record session frequency (sessions per week and minutes per session)
- Store and view IEP goals for each student
- Track multiple assessment types (STAR Reading, STAR Math, WISC-V, BRIEF, WIAT-4, WJ-IV, mClass)
- View student progress dashboards with trend analysis

**Why it matters:** Everything you need to know about your caseload is in one place—no more juggling spreadsheets and paper files.

---

### 2. Smart Scheduling

A visual, drag-and-drop scheduling system built for special education workflows.

**What you can do:**

- View schedules by day, week, or month
- Drag and drop sessions onto a visual calendar
- Automatically detect scheduling conflicts
- Integrate with school bell schedules to avoid pulling students during restricted times
- Account for special class activities (PE, Library, Music, etc.) when scheduling
- Mark school holidays and breaks
- Group multiple students into shared sessions
- Export schedules to PDF for printing or sharing
- Assign sessions to SEAs or other support staff

**Why it matters:** Scheduling around classroom activities, recess, and other commitments is one of the most time-consuming parts of the job. Speddy handles the complexity so you can focus on students.

---

### 3. AI-Powered Lesson & Worksheet Generation

Create customized, IEP-aligned learning materials in seconds.

**What you can do:**

- Generate worksheets and lesson plans tailored to individual student needs
- Input student profile, grade level, subject, and IEP goals
- Receive two-part output: a printable student worksheet and a detailed lesson plan
- Adjust difficulty level based on student performance
- Save worksheets for future use
- Print directly or generate QR codes for digital access

**Why it matters:** Creating individualized materials for each student is incredibly time-consuming. Speddy's AI generates high-quality, goal-aligned materials in seconds—not hours.

---

### 4. Exit Tickets

Quick formative assessments tied directly to IEP goals.

**What you can do:**

- Create short assessments (exit tickets) aligned to specific IEP goals
- Generate exit tickets for multiple students at once
- Grade student responses and mark problems as correct, incorrect, or excluded
- Track student progress toward IEP goals over time
- Print exit tickets for paper-based administration

**Why it matters:** Quick, targeted assessments help you understand what students are learning during each session and provide documentation for IEP progress monitoring.

---

### 5. Progress Checks

Comprehensive assessments for deeper progress monitoring.

**What you can do:**

- Create in-depth assessments covering multiple skills
- Generate assessments aligned to student IEP goals
- Grade individual questions and track per-goal performance
- View detailed results with question-by-question analysis
- Monitor student growth over time

**Why it matters:** When IEP review time comes, you'll have documented evidence of student progress across multiple assessment points.

---

### 6. Curriculum Tracking

Track where each student is in their instructional sequence.

**Supported Curriculums:**

- **SPIRE Reading Program** (Foundations through Level 8)
- **Reveal Math** (Grades K-5)

**What you can do:**

- Record current lesson or level for each student
- Track curriculum progress at the session level
- Maintain consistent instruction across multiple service providers

**Why it matters:** When multiple staff work with the same student, everyone stays on the same page about where instruction left off.

---

### 7. Bell Schedules & Special Activities

Coordinate with general education schedules to minimize classroom disruption.

**Bell Schedules:**

- Define school start/end times by grade level
- Set restricted times (recess, lunch, PE, specials)
- Import bell schedules in bulk via CSV

**Special Activities:**

- Record teacher-specific class activities (Library, STEAM, Music, Art, PE)
- Prevent scheduling conflicts with important classroom activities
- Teachers can add their own activities for specialists to see

**Why it matters:** Pulling students during important classroom activities creates problems for everyone. Speddy helps you find the right times automatically.

---

### 8. Calendar & Event Management

A full calendar system for managing your professional schedule.

**What you can do:**

- View your schedule by day, week, or month
- Add meetings, assessments, and other events
- Mark school holidays to exclude from scheduling
- Export weekly schedules to PDF
- Navigate easily between time periods

**Why it matters:** Your professional calendar and student session schedule live in one place, giving you a complete picture of your day.

---

### 9. Team Management

Coordinate with SEAs and other support staff.

**What you can do:**

- View and manage assigned SEAs
- Share SEAs with other specialists at your school
- Assign SEAs to specific student sessions
- See which staff are assigned to which sessions

**Why it matters:** When multiple adults support your caseload, coordination is essential. Speddy keeps everyone aligned.

---

### 10. Multi-Site Support

For specialists who work at multiple schools.

**What you can do:**

- Maintain separate caseloads at each school
- Set your work schedule for each site (which days you're at which school)
- Switch between schools easily within the app
- Manage all your work from one account

**Why it matters:** Itinerant specialists no longer need separate systems for each school they serve.

---

### 11. Teacher Portal

Give general education teachers visibility into resource services.

**What teachers can see:**

- Which of their students receive resource services
- When students are pulled for sessions
- Student IEP goals (appropriate access)

**What teachers can do:**

- Add special class activities that specialists can see when scheduling
- Coordinate with the resource team

**Why it matters:** Better communication between classroom teachers and specialists improves outcomes for students.

---

### 12. Administrator Dashboard

Tools for site and district administrators.

**What administrators can do:**

- View and manage teacher accounts
- Create new staff accounts
- Identify and resolve duplicate records
- View school-wide staff counts and information
- Manage school data and assignments

**Why it matters:** Administrators need oversight without getting into the day-to-day details. Speddy provides the right level of visibility.

---

### 13. Progress Tracking & Reporting

Document student growth with meaningful data.

**What you can do:**

- View progress charts showing student performance over time
- Track advancement toward IEP goals
- See subject-specific trends (Reading, Math, Writing, Spelling, Phonics)
- Identify students who are improving, stable, or need additional support
- Access assessment history in one place

**Why it matters:** IEP progress reporting requires documentation. Speddy collects the data as you work, so reporting is simple when you need it.

---

### 14. Session Documents

Attach resources to sessions for easy access.

**What you can do:**

- Attach PDFs, links, and notes to individual sessions or groups
- Upload files directly to sessions
- Keep session-specific materials organized

**Why it matters:** Everything you need for a session is accessible in one place.

---

### 15. Saved Worksheets Library

Build a personal library of reusable materials.

**What you can do:**

- Save generated worksheets for future use
- Browse and search your saved materials
- Regenerate worksheets with one click

**Why it matters:** Once you create something that works, you shouldn't have to create it again.
